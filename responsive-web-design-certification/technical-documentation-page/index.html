Resistor

A Resistor may be one of the most pasic electronic components; it restrics the flow of electricity by introducing electrical resistance. Think of restricting the flow in a garden hose by pinching it, that is basically it.

Various types of resistors exist, each with their own advantages and disadvantages:

  • Carbon Type Resistors
  • Metal Film Resistors
  • Wire Wound Resistors
  • Specialty Resistors

Switch

A switch is a simple "diversion point" for the electric current: It can allow for current to flow or inhibit it. It can also divert the current onto one of two paths, like a railway switch.

The most basic form of switch is the SPST switch, standing for Single Pole Single Throw: A single point of contact can be thrown a single way, so there is no choice: It's either the one circuit or none (off).

Transistor

A transistor is a type of electronic switch, utilizing enriched or depleted fields to enable signal switching.

LED

The Light Emitting Diode (LED) can produce light! Wikipedia says:

A light-emitting diode (LED) is a semiconductor device that emits light when current flows through it. Electrons in the semiconductor recombine with electron holes, releasing energy in the form of photons. The color of the light (corresponding to the energy of the photons) is determined by the energy required for electrons to cross the band gap of the semiconductor. White light is obtained by using multiple semiconductors or a layer of light-emitting phosphor on the semiconductor device.
